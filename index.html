How to Play the Pig Game

The objective is to be the first player to reach 100 points!

  • Roll the dice to accumulate points for your turn.
  • If you roll a 1, your current score resets, and your turn ends.
  • You can choose to "Hold" and add your current score to your total score.
  • The first player to reach 100 total points wins!
